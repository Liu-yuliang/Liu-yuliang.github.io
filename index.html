Ph.D. Student, Nanjing University | Jointly trained at Shanghai Innovation Institute

I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University, jointly trained at Shanghai Innovation Institute, where I am advised by Zhouhan Lin. Previously, I worked with Qing Gu and Zhiwei Jiang at Nanjing University. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2021.

I have worked on almost the full LLM pipeline, including evaluation, post-training, long-context modeling, reasoning, and efficient inference. Since 2025, I have focused primarily on pre-training, which I view as the foundation of model capability.

Biography

My recent research centers on large language model pre-training and capability development. I am particularly interested in how better data, objectives, and training recipes can improve downstream reasoning and generalization.

More broadly, I enjoy building a full-stack understanding of language models, from how we train them, to how we evaluate them, to how we make them useful in real systems.
